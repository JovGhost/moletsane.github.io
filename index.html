ABOUT
Moletsane Foundation is a registered Non-profit organization established with a primary focus on impacting educational outcomes through improved nutrition and self-reliance programmess. Moletsane Foundation reaches over 100 kids in areas of ward 21 (Moletsane and Tladi) alleviating poverty, setting up and organising community and individual feeding initiatives and after school developement programmes.

VISION
To especially address malnutrition, poverty, hunger and play a leading role in empowering the community with skills that leads to self-reliance and independence.

MISSION
To uplift disadvantaged communities with a particular focus on children's health, growth, developement and wellbeing.
